---
title: Getting Started
description: First steps after installing renCal.
---

After installing renCal, open the app and connect your first calendar account.

## 1. Open renCal

Launch **renCal** from your app launcher.

The main window shows your calendar in month, week, or agenda-style views depending on the current layout.

## 2. Connect a calendar

Open settings and add a calendar provider.

renCal supports calendars through Caldir providers, including Google Calendar, iCloud, Outlook, and CalDAV servers.

Once connected, renCal syncs your events into a local caldir directory as `.ics` files.

## 3. Create an event

Use the event input to create events naturally:

```txt
lunch with Sarah tomorrow at 1pm
```

renCal will parse the text and turn it into a calendar event.

## 4. Navigate with the keyboard

renCal is designed to be keyboard-friendly. Use the built-in shortcuts to move around quickly and manage events without leaving the keyboard.

Press <kbd>?</kbd> in the app to see available shortcuts.

## 5. Customize the app

Open settings to adjust preferences such as:

- connected calendars,
- themes,
- notifications,
- and other app behavior.
